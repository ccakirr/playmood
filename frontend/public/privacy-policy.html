Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

PlayMood uses AI to generate music playlists. When you use our YouTube integration:

  • We request access to your YouTube account to create playlists
  • We do not store your Google credentials
  • We do not access your personal YouTube data beyond playlist creation

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Generate AI-powered music playlists based on your prompts
  • Create playlists on your YouTube account (only when you explicitly request)
  • Search for videos to add to your playlists

3. Data Storage

  • Generated playlists are temporarily stored in memory during the session
  • We do not permanently store your playlist data
  • We do not store your YouTube credentials

4. Third-Party Services

PlayMood integrates with:

  • Google/YouTube API: For creating and managing playlists
  • HuggingFace: For AI-powered playlist generation

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke YouTube access at any time via your Google Account Permissions
  • Delete playlists created by PlayMood directly from YouTube

6. Security

We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. We never see or store your password.
